About Us

Our Mission

We’re here to develop the best education in the world and make it universally available. Our global team works together to make language learning fun, free, and effective for anyone who wants to learn, wherever they are.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: We are dedicated to making language education accessible to everyone, regardless of economic background or location.
  • Innovation: We continuously strive to develop cutting-edge technology to enhance language learning experiences.
  • Impact: We measure success by the positive impact we have on learners worldwide.
